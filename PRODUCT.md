# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Internal operators and admins who need a task-focused management system. They usually sign in before reviewing dashboards, companies, orders, and users.

## Product Purpose
Provide a clean authenticated entry point and a foundation for an admin dashboard. Success means users can register, log in, receive clear validation feedback, and land in a navigation shell that can grow with future modules.

## Brand Personality
Clear, dependable, composed. The interface should feel like a serious operations tool, not a marketing page.

## Anti-references
Avoid decorative SaaS landing-page composition, oversized hero copy, noisy gradients, and card-heavy layouts that slow down routine admin work.

## Design Principles
1. Keep authentication feedback explicit and actionable.
2. Use familiar product patterns so operators can move quickly.
3. Make the navigation shell stable and easy to extend.
4. Prioritize readability, contrast, and predictable form behavior.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Respect reduced-motion preferences and avoid relying on color alone for errors or success states.
